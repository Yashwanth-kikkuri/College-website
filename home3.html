ABOUT US

Today, Tommorow, Together

Indian Institute of Information Technology Chittoor at Sri City, known as IIITS, is an institute of national importance and a pinnacle of technology education standards in India. The Institute is governed by distinguished academicians, industry leaders and senior government officials, referred to as the Board of Governors. Additionally, IIIT Hyderabad and IIT Hyderabad are the mentor institutions for IIIT Sri City. In fact, The Institute follows a similar curriculum and pedagogy as IIIT Hyderabad.

IIITS was established under the Government of India’s Initiative to set up 20 Institutes across India, focused on Information Technology. The Institute was setup by the Government of India – MHRD, Government of Andhra Pradesh and Industry Partners (Sri City Foundation) as a Not-for-Profit Public-Private-Partnership. The Institute was funded 50 percent by the Government of India, 35 percent by the Government of Andhra Pradesh and 15 percent by the Industry Partners.

Our Campus spread across 80 acres and equipped with state-of-the-art Infrastructure is coming up in Sri City. The Campus will match international standards, while keeping in mind the culture and values of India. Sri City is an emerging Smart City and the hub for over 100 MNC’s from 25 Countries.

Indian Institute of Information Technology at Sri City offers the following Undergraduate Programs, which are focused on Information Technology:

Bachelor in Technology with Major in Computer Science and Engineering (200 Seats) Bachelor in Technology with Major in Electronics and Communication Engineering (70 Seats)

IIITS follows a similar curriculum as IIIT-Hyderabad and leading American and European Universities. Unlike regular Engineering Institutions which follow class-room based learning and examination based grading, we focus on problem-solving, real-world projects and group education at IIITS. The Institute provides its students the opportunity to select courses and projects of their Choice. The Institute also allows provides its students the opportunity to participate in variety of research, technology development and community Programs. Overall, The Institute curriculum is modern, competitive and demands hard-work from each Student. Note that admission to Undergraduate Programs is done once a year during the Monsoon Semester.

What does one look for in a College? Education, Placement. In addition, we at IIITS believe in providing our students with opportunities for All-Round Development. We encourage our students to conduct research, lead community projects, build startups, develop new products and services, become the next arts or sports star, Name It! IIITS is truly a revolution in the field of it education in India. Our faculty believes that in addition to textbooks, research and real-life experiences are the new keystones for Education. So, ask yourself are you up for the Challenge?

Our campus constitutes of first-grade infrastructure and is set to be opened in Sri City. In the meanwhile, the institute is functioning from the campus of Institute of Financial Management Research at Sri City.
